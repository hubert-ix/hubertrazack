About Hubert Razack

Hubert Razack has been involved in the music industry for the best part of two decades. Born in France, he moved in London, UK, to pursue a music career and produced a number of artists as well as his own projects.

Relocated in Toronto since 2006, he has composed music for media, concert series, live shows, as well as continuing his music production business.

Although is now focusing on his composition work, Hubert is also an accomplished tenor and can be found performing in and around Toronto.
